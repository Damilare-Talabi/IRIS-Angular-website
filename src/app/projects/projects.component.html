E-passport

ISTL entered into a Public Private Partnership with the Federal Republic
of Nigeria for upgrading the Nigeria travel document to an internationally
compliant passport in 2003. The project has been running successfully
since 2007 with over 9 million e-passports issued.

Major Achievments
