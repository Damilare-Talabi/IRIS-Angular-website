Public Key Infrastructure (PKI)

ISTL implemented the Nigerian e-Passport PKI which conforms to ICAO standards.

Our infrastructure includes a trusted CA for identity verification, the use of HSMs

to generate, store, and handle key pairs, as well as participation in

ICAO Public Key Directory.
